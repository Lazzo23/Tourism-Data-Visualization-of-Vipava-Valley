Tourism Data Visualization of Vipava Valley

Nestled between the Karst Plateau and the Trnovo Forest, the Vipava Valley is one of Slovenia’s most diverse and inviting regions. Shaped by the gentle Vipava River and sheltered by surrounding hills, it enjoys a mild Mediterranean climate that nurtures vineyards, orchards, and olive groves. Tourism in the valley thrives on its natural beauty, wine culture, and outdoor activities, from hiking and cycling to paragliding above the emerald landscapes.
This visualization explores tourism dynamics across six municipalities that form the heart of the valley: Ajdovščina, Vipava, Nova Gorica, Miren-Kostanjevica, Renče-Vogrsko, and Šempeter-Vrtojba. Each community offers a unique blend of tradition and innovation, contributing to the region’s growing appeal among both domestic and international visitors.
