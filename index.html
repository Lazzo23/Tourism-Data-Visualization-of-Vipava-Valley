Tourism Data Visualization of Vipava Valley

The Vipava Valley is shaped by a mild Mediterranean climate and diverse landscapes that support rich outdoor and cultural tourism. This visualization presents key tourism patterns across six municipalities: Ajdovščina, Vipava, Nova Gorica, Miren-Kostanjevica, Renče-Vogrsko, and Šempeter-Vrtojba. It highlights monthly arrivals, overnight stays, and related weather data, allowing users to explore how different factors influence tourism dynamics in the region.
